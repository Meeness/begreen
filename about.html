About Us

Paper Water Bottle products are designedto address a growing requirement for responsible production, sustainable consumption, and low-impact disposal.
Today, our products are made of 65% compostable material with improvements being added regularly. The remainder is landfill biodegradable* and recyclable. Our goal is to be as near 100% compostable as possible. Join us, as we discover and apply better ways to make sustainable bottles. Together, we will help save our planet!
